📧 Before We Begin

To get started with Datadog, please complete the following steps:

1
Check your email for a Datadog invitation. The invitation should have been sent to your company email address.
2
Accept the invitation and create your Datadog account using the link provided in the email.
3
Set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to secure your account. This is required for all team members and helps protect our monitoring infrastructure.

⚠️ Important: Please complete these steps before we proceed with the workshop. If you haven't received your invitation or need assistance, please let us know!

📋 Workshop Agenda

Today we'll cover the essential Datadog features you'll use in your daily work.

1
📚 Documentation
Overview of Datadog documentation, where to find resources, and how to navigate the knowledge base effectively.
2
🔍 Error, Metrics & Logs Explorer
Learn how to search, filter, and analyze errors, metrics, and logs. We'll cover query syntax, visualization options, and common troubleshooting workflows.
3
📊 Application Performance Monitoring (APM)
Dive into distributed tracing, service maps, and performance profiling. Understand how to identify bottlenecks and optimize application performance.
4
🤖 Monitoring & Bits AI
Set up monitors, alerts, and notifications. Explore Bits AI for intelligent anomaly detection and automated insights into your infrastructure.
